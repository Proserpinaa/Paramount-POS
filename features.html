Tipping

Increase Your Revenue

Our POS interface has pre-set tip options right on the screen, taking the guess work out of tipping. Now customers are more inclined to tip, with a simple tap of a button. Your staff will feel more appreciated and you will increase your revenue!

Made to Order

You can customize tip settings – create specific tip amounts depending on your business type or use the default tip options that we already have available. Patrons also have the option of entering a custom tip amount or even doing it the old fashioned way when signing their printed receipt.

Keep the Change

Settle Tip Feature allows your employees to quickly tap and enter tip amounts from printed receipts, view/settle any open tickets then calculate overall sales and quickly go through end of shift checkout. Supervisors can view and edit all employee tip amounts at any time and shift summary report shows exactly what is owed.

Security

Protecting your customers is our top priority!

Our POS System is integrated with only the best EMV compliant equipment engineered to safeguard your customer’s data with the best security technology.

Our card readers encrypt payment information at the point of sale to keep valuable information safe while processing and transferring the data for end-to-end payment protection.

Paramount POS fully manages and regularly installs security updates on its servers and all applications are verified with rigorous quality testing and review.

Paramount POS gives you peace of mind knowing that your customer’s data is secure and confident that you’re protected against data breaches.

We also adhere to Level 1 PCI Data Security Standards (PCI-DSS) to keep valuable information safe and never store data on your device.
